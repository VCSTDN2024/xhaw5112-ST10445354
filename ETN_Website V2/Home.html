Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation was founded in 2016 with a vision to uplift and enhance the skills of domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Our mission is to provide valuable, hands-on training that will allow workers to gain new, marketable skills, improving their job prospects and personal growth. We offer both six-week and six-month programs tailored to meet the unique needs of our participants, ensuring that they are equipped with the knowledge and confidence to excel in their chosen fields.
